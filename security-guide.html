Cyber Security Guide

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security is the practice of protecting computers, servers, networks, mobile devices and data from unauthorized access, attacks and theft.

Password Security Rules

Use passwords with at least 12 characters. Include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers and special symbols. Never reuse passwords on multiple websites.

Two Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA adds an extra layer of security by requiring a second verification step such as OTP, Authenticator App or Security Key.

Password Managers

Password managers help users store and manage strong passwords securely. Examples include Bitwarden, 1Password and LastPass.

Safe Browsing

Always visit trusted websites, check HTTPS, avoid suspicious downloads and never click unknown links from emails or messages.

Cyber Awareness

Stay informed about phishing, malware, ransomware and social engineering attacks. Cyber awareness is the first line of defense.
