Common Cyber Attacks

Phishing Attack

Fake emails, websites or messages are used to trick users into revealing passwords, banking details or personal information.

Malware Attack

Malicious software enters a device and damages files, steals data or controls the system.

Ransomware

Attackers encrypt files and demand money to restore access to important data.

Spyware

Spyware secretly monitors user activity and collects sensitive information without consent.

DDoS Attack

A server is flooded with massive traffic, making websites and online services unavailable.

Social Engineering

Attackers manipulate people psychologically to gain unauthorized access to systems.

How To Stay Safe?

Use strong passwords, enable 2FA, update software regularly, avoid suspicious links and always verify unknown emails before clicking.
